# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We as members, contributors, and maintainers pledge to make participation in the
AgentLedger community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age,
body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender
identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status,
nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse,
inclusive, and healthy community.

## Our standards

This project adopts the **[Contributor Covenant, version 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/)**
as its code of conduct. Please read it for the full set of expected behaviors,
unacceptable behaviors, enforcement responsibilities, scope, and enforcement
guidelines. The canonical text and translations are available at
<https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/>.

In short: be respectful, assume good faith, give and accept constructive feedback
gracefully, and focus on what is best for the community.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported
to the project maintainers at **shekhar.nik@gmail.com**. All complaints will be
reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. Maintainers are obligated to respect
the privacy and security of the reporter of any incident.

Maintainers who do not follow or enforce this Code of Conduct in good faith may face
temporary or permanent repercussions as determined by other members of the project's
leadership.
